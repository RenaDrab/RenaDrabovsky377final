How to find food near you:

On the home page where you see "Search for healthy food in your zipcode!" there will be a place to enter zipcode, item and to choose category(circled in red down bellow). Input your zip code, item you are looking for and click the search button. The organic category will show you only organic versions of your item. The nutritius category will show you versions of your item that are by different brands and it will display a Nutrition score based on data from Kroger. Where you see N/A do not panic, this just means that data is unavalible from Kroger at this time.

Helpful Tips:

IF YOU LEAVE ZIPCODE OR ITEM BLANK YOU WILL GET A MESSAGE ASKING YOU TO ENTER THEM(shown in purple bellow)

You can only enter a valid zipcode, not just random numbers.

If there are no Kroger stores in your zipcode then nothing will show up. For example: I tried a florida Sarasota zipcode but there was no data for any locations there so nothing popped up, I tried the same thing with a few zipcodes around Maryland and the information showed up correctly.

You can only enter 5 numbers for zipcode.

Make sure to spell the food item correctly.

If nothing pops up check your spelling and if that doesnt work Kroger might not have data of your item availible at this time.


How to look up ratings correctly:

On the home page where you see "compare by brand, type and size," you should see where it says enter item(circled in red bellow), Enter desired item and click "Rate!" Different versions of the item will pop up, it will display the brand name, specific item and its rating to tell you which items people actually like over others.

Helpful Tips:

Spell item correctly or nothing will show up

If you spell correctly and item still doesnt shown up, Kroger might not have data of your item availible at this time.


How to do a nutrition search:

On the home page where you see "Help meet your nutrition goals!" Input your item where it says enter item (circled in red bellow) and click "Find". This will show different versions of your item with nutrition facts so you can compare them and make the best decision.

Helpful Tips:

Spell item correctly or nothing will show up

If you spell correctly and item still doesnt shown up, Kroger might not have data of your item availible at this time.


How to navigate about page:

On the home page on the top middle of the page you will see a navigation bar and a button that says about. If you click that it will take you to the about page. There if you click "show calories in bread comparison," it will open a chart comparing the calories of bread for different brands. Under that you will see a bunch of questions that are buttons(circled in red bellow). If you click any of them, it will open up the answer to that question bellow all the buttons.

Helpful Tips:

When clicking on a question make sure to scroll all the way down to see the answer
